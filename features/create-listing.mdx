---
title: "Creating a Listing"
description: "Publish a new item or service to the TruequeU marketplace for other students to find."
---

<Warning>
  You must be logged in to create a listing. If you visit `/create` without an active session, TruequeU will show a prompt directing you to log in.
</Warning>

To publish a new listing, navigate to `/create` or click **+ New Listing** from your profile page.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the create listing page">
    Go to `/create` directly, or open your profile at `/profile` and click the **+ New Listing** button in the top-right of your profile card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter a title">
    Type a descriptive title for your item in the **Item Title** field. A good title helps other students find your listing through search.

    Example: *Calculus Stewart 8th Edition*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a price">
    Enter the price in USD in the **Price ($)** field. You can set it to `0` for a free item or giveaway. Prices up to `$999,999` are accepted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a category">
    Select one of the four categories from the **Category** dropdown:
    - **Electronics** — Devices and accessories
    - **Books** — Textbooks and course materials
    - **Services** — Skills and services offered to other students
    - **Other** — Everything else
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write a description">
    Describe your item in the **Description** field. Include condition, reason for selling, and any extras included (e.g., case, charger, accessories). Descriptions must be at least 10 characters.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload a product image (optional)">
    Click **Browse files** or drag and drop an image into the upload area. TruequeU reads the file using the browser's `FileReader` API and stores it as a base64 data URL — no external upload required.

    If you skip this step, a placeholder image will be used automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish the listing">
    Click **Publish Listing**. TruequeU will validate your inputs, add the listing to the store, and redirect you to the home page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Validation rules

| Field | Minimum | Maximum | Required |
|---|---|---|---|
| Title | 3 characters | 100 characters | Yes |
| Price | $0 | $999,999 | Yes |
| Description | 10 characters | 1,000 characters | Yes |
| Image | — | — | No |
| Category | — | — | Yes (defaults to Electronics) |

<Note>
  After publishing, your listing is immediately added to the top of the featured section on the home page and visible to all students browsing `/listings`.
</Note>
