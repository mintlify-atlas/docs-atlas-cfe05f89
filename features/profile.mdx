<Note>
  Your profile is only visible when you are logged in. Visiting `/profile` without a session shows a sign-in prompt with links to `/login` and `/signup`.
</Note>

Your profile page lives at `/profile` and gives you a central place to see your account details and manage your listings.

## Accessing your profile

Navigate to `/profile` from the navbar. If you are logged in, the page loads your profile card immediately.

## What your profile shows

**Profile card** — at the top of the page, your profile card displays:

- **Avatar** — A unique avatar generated by [DiceBear Avataaars](https://www.dicebear.com/styles/avataaars/) using your user ID as the seed. Every user gets a consistent, distinct avatar.
- **Name** — Your display name as registered.
- **Email** — Your university email address.

**My Listings** — below the profile card, all listings you have published are shown in the same grid layout used on the main listings page. If you haven't published anything yet, a prompt with a **Create your first listing** link is shown instead.

## Creating a listing from your profile

Click the **+ New Listing** button in the top-right corner of your profile card. This navigates to `/create`, where you can fill out the listing form.

## Signing out

Click the **Sign Out** button next to **+ New Listing** in your profile card. This clears your session from the Zustand store (sets `user` to `null`) and returns you to the unauthenticated state. Your previously published listings remain visible in the marketplace.
