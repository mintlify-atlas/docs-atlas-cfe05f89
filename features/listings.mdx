---
title: "Browsing Listings"
description: "Explore and search all items available for trade or sale in the TruequeU marketplace."
---

The listings page shows every item available in the TruequeU community — both seed items and listings created by other students. Navigate to `/listings` from the navbar to access it.

## Searching listings

The search bar in the navbar filters listings in real time by title. As you type, only listings whose titles match your query are shown. The filter is case-insensitive.

<Tip>
  Search is matched against the listing title only. If you don't find what you need, try broader terms — for example, search "calc" instead of "Calculus Stewart 8th Edition".
</Tip>

If no listings match your query, the page shows: *"No listings match your search. Try adjusting your search terms."*

## Listing statuses

Every listing has one of three statuses, displayed as a badge on the listing card.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Available" icon="circle-check">
    The item is actively listed and open for contact. Buyers can message the seller directly from the card or detail page.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Reserved" icon="clock">
    The seller has tentatively agreed to a transaction with a buyer. The listing is still visible but may not be available.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sold" icon="circle-xmark">
    The item has been traded or sold. The listing remains visible for reference but is no longer active.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Other" icon="tag">
    Some listings may use custom statuses set by the seller to reflect the item's current state.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Categories

Listings are organized into four categories:

- **Electronics** — Laptops, tablets, phones, accessories, and other devices.
- **Books** — Textbooks, course materials, and study guides.
- **Services** — Tutoring, design work, repairs, and other student-offered services.
- **Other** — Anything that doesn't fit the above categories.

You can see a listing's category on its detail page, displayed above the title.

## Viewing a listing

Click **Details** on any listing card to open the full listing detail page at `/listing/:id`. The detail page shows:

- The item's image gallery (with thumbnail navigation)
- Title, price, category, and status
- Full description
- Seller information (name and university email domain)
- A **Contact Seller** button to open a direct chat
- A **Make an Offer** button
