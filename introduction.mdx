---
title: "Introduction"
description: "TruequeU is a peer-to-peer marketplace built for university students to buy, sell, and trade items within their campus community."
---

TruequeU connects university students who want to exchange goods — from textbooks and electronics to furniture and clothing — in a trusted, campus-focused environment. Students can list items, browse what others are offering, chat directly with sellers, and manage their own listings from a personal profile.

## Key features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Student marketplace" icon="store">
    Browse and list items for sale or trade with other students at your university. Listings include photos, descriptions, pricing, and availability status.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Listings" icon="tag">
    Create detailed listings with a title, description, category, price, and images. Track each listing's status: available, reserved, or sold.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Real-time chat" icon="message-circle">
    Contact sellers directly through the built-in chat system. Each listing has a dedicated chat thread between buyer and seller.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Favorites" icon="heart">
    Save listings you're interested in by toggling the favorite button. Revisit saved items from your browsing history.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Profile" icon="user">
    Manage your account and view all your active, reserved, and sold listings from your profile page at `/profile`.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Search" icon="search">
    Filter listings in real time using the search bar in the navbar. Results update as you type to match listing titles and descriptions.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  TruequeU uses your browser's localStorage to persist all data — listings, user sessions, and chat messages — between page refreshes. No external database is required to run the app.
</Note>
