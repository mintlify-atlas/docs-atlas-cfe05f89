# Welcome to TruequeU

TruequeU is a peer-to-peer marketplace built exclusively for university students. Browse, post, and trade items — from textbooks to laptops — directly with your campus community. No middlemen, no fees, just students helping students.

## Why TruequeU?

University life means constantly buying new textbooks, upgrading equipment, and accumulating items you no longer need. TruequeU connects you with fellow students who want exactly what you have — and who have exactly what you need.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Sign up with your name and email. No university email required — just a valid password meeting our security requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse or search listings">
    Explore items posted by the community. Use the search bar to find exactly what you're looking for.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact the seller">
    Found something you want? Click **Contact Seller** on any listing to open a direct chat.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Post your own items">
    Have something to sell or trade? Create a listing in seconds with a title, description, price, and photo.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key features

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| **Listings marketplace** | Browse all available items or filter by search query |
| **Listing creation** | Post items with images, categories, and pricing |
| **In-app chat** | Message directly with buyers and sellers |
| **Favorites** | Save listings you're interested in |
| **Profile management** | Manage all your posted listings in one place |
| **Persistent state** | Your data is saved locally — no account sync needed |

<Note>
  TruequeU stores all data locally in your browser using localStorage. Your listings, chats, and account information persist between sessions on the same device.
</Note>
